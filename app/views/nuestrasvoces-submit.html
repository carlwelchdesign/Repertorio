METLIFE NUESTRAS VOCES NATIONAL PLAYWRITING COMPETITION 2015

Requirements

Plays that do not meet these requirements will not be considered (please read carefully).

  1. Playwrights may be Latino or of any other ethnic or racial background as long as the play's subject matter and characters resonate with Latino audiences and accurately depict the Hispanic experience.
  2. No screenplays, one act plays, musicals, adaptations or translations will be accepted.
  3. New and un-produced plays preferred. Plays that have had readings or a workshop production are acceptable.
  4. All plays must be original and full-length (minimum running time: 1 hr., 30 min) and can be written in Spanish and/or English.
  5. Playwrights must be at least 18 years of age and residents of the United States or Puerto Rico.
  6. Previously submitted scripts are accepted unless they have already placed in the top 10.

Selection Process

  1. Each script will be read by two qualified independent judges selected by Repertorio Español, on a blind submission.
  2. Winners will be announced six to seven months after the deadline.
  3. Each participant will be notified of the results by e-mail. We request that participants do not contact the office to inquire about the competition’s results.

Prizes

Round 1: Finalists will receive a staged reading at Repertorio to further develop the script.

Round 2:

Grand Prize Winner: $3,000 and a full production at Repertorio Español (must be available for Awards Ceremony)

2nd Place: $2,000

3rd Place: $1,000
