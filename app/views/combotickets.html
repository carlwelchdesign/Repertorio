What are the perks?

  1. Combotickets are for all Repertorio Español regular season shows, as well as select Special Guest Presentations.
  2. Comboticket buyers will receive best seats available within their respective subscription purchase.
  3. Comboticket holders receive up to (2) complementary servings of wine per ticket at each presentation.
  4. Comboticket holders will receive a monthly email reminding them of their unused tickets.
  5. Comboticket holders will receive advance notices of upcoming available seats for Special Guest Presentations.
  6. Comboticket holders may purchase regular tickets in another area, without having to redeem their COMBO ticket(s), If no tickets are available on a desired date and in their respective subscription seating area.
  7. Comboticket holders receive two (2) free tickets to any Van Lier Director’s Production.
  8. Comboticket holders will receive a 25% discount on Repertorio published books, CDs and DVDs.

What is the policy?

  1. Combotickets may only be redeemed for a maximum of (2) seats per production, for (3) regular season shows offered within the year from the time of subscription purchase, or (1) seat for six regular season shows.
  2. Comboticket buyers must reserve their seats at least 48 hours in advance of the desired performance date.
  3. Combotickets may not be combined with other discount offers.
  4. COMBO subscription tickets cannot be used for COMBO PREMIUM subscription seating area without a purchased upgrade.

How do I redeem my combotickets?

Combotickets can only be redeemed by phone or email. Call our Box Office at 212.225.9999 or email us at tickets@repetorio.org
