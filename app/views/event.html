Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Repertorio Español located?

Repertorio is located at 138 East 27th Street in Manhattan between Lexington and Third Avenues. The closest MTA stations are located on 28th Street, which include the 6, N, & R trains. If traveling by car from New Jersey, please plan ahead as delays are very common.

If I’m coming by car, where can I park?

The closest parking garage is Icon Parking located on East 29th Street between Lexington and Third Avenues. Parking must be validated at the theater’s box office in order to receive the discounted parking rate of $15 for 4 hours of parking.

Are your productions appropriate for children?

Repertorio does not permit anyone under the age of 12 in the theater with the exception of those attending a specified children’s production. Some subject matter may not be suitable for those under 18, so please contact us in advance with any concerns.

I can’t make the show. Can I change my ticket?

Repertorio does not allow for exchanges or refunds once tickets are purchased. Refunds and exchanges can only be made in the event of a performance cancelation in which you will be notified immediately.

What restaurants are near to Repertorio?

Repertorio is located near many restaurants including several West Asian and Middle Eastern style cuisine. Check out Yelp to see a listing of recommended restaurants in our area.

Is there translation available for all of your productions?

No. There are some productions where translation is not available. Please contact us when purchasing tickets to inquire if translation is available for your desired performance date.
